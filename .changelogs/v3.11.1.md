## Changed

- Review loops (`local-agent-review-loop`, `ollama-review-loop`): collapsed the `timeout`/`gtimeout`/none wrapper selection into a single deterministic one-liner folded into the pre-flight block, and added explicit "run it, don't narrate it" directives. Stops the reviewing agent from reasoning aloud about whether `timeout` is installed on macOS on every codex/ollama review.
- `ollama-review-loop`: made the model's review output reliably parseable — isolate stderr from the findings log, hide reasoning-model thinking, and emit schema-constrained JSON. Gate the `--hidethinking`/`--nowordwrap`/`--format` flags behind capability probes so older `ollama` versions don't error, and treat exit-0 empty model responses as a coverage gap rather than a clean pass.
