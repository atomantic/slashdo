# Release v3.17.2

Released: 2026-06-26

## Highlights
- **`do:next` issue auto-pick now respects ordering.** Two opt-in, backward-compatible controls let you express the order in which `--issues` mode claims work, instead of the previous strict oldest-first walk: a `Depends on #N` / `Blocked by #N` body line (or GitHub's native blocked-by relationship) holds an issue back until its blocker closes and surfaces it automatically afterward, and a `priority:<N>` label nudges an independent issue earlier or later. Both are no-ops when unused, so existing plans are unaffected.
- **`do:replan` understands the new markers.** A blocked issue is never pruned as stale for inactivity, and a new dependency/priority pass keeps markers clean — stripping a satisfied `Depends on #N` once `#N` closes and flagging broken references and cycles.
- **Infra & deps.** Bumped `actions/checkout` from 6 to 7 in the release workflow.

## Changed
- `do:next` `--issues` auto-pick orders candidates by `[priorityRank, createdAt]` and skips issues with any open hard dependency (`commands/do/next.md`).
- `do:replan` keeps blocked issues and gained a dependency/priority marker-hygiene pass (`commands/do/replan.md`).

## Dependencies
- Bump `actions/checkout` from 6 to 7 (#90).
